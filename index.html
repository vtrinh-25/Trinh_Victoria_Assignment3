Hi! I’m Victoria Trinh, a Chinese-Vietnamese Toronto-based designer and visual storyteller with a passion for clean, modern design combined with the expressive aesthetics of Asian visual culture. Currently studying Global Business and Digital Arts from the University of Waterloo, I blend creativity with strategy to craft visuals that not only look beautiful, but communicate with clarity and purpose.

My work spans graphic design, UI/UX, cinematography, and marketing, and I especially love working in the industries of music and technology. Whether I’m shaping a brand identity, designing intuitive interfaces, or framing a shot, I’m driven by detail, intention, and a playful curiosity that keeps my ideas unique.

Outside of design, you can usually find me on a volleyball court, behind a camera, or deep into K-pop, K-dramas, and C-dramas. Photography, videography, and storytelling fuel my creative energy and inspire the way I see light, colour, and emotion in my work. I’m also someone who values meaningful connections. I’m incredibly close with my family and friends, and they’re a big part of what keeps me grounded and inspired.
